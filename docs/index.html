About

About Deepwater Daily

An automated energy news aggregator focused on Gulf of America deepwater and oil and gas activity.

What it tracks

Deepwater Daily monitors offshore drilling, Gulf operations, crude oil, natural gas, LNG, subsea systems, pipelines, wells, and exploration news.

How it works

A scheduled collector fetches industry feeds, filters for deepwater and energy relevance, categorizes each item, and publishes a static CSV-backed site.

Sources

Sources include Offshore Magazine, Rigzone, World Oil, Oil Price, Offshore Technology, Oil and Gas Journal, EIA, BOEM, BSEE, and operator newsrooms.

Why this layout

The interface mirrors the Second Brain reading pattern: persistent navigation, immediate search, compact filters, and dense cards designed for scanning.
