About Deepwater Daily

What is Deepwater Daily?

Deepwater Daily is an automated news aggregator focused on the Gulf of America deepwater drilling, oil & gas exploration, and energy industry. We collect and curate the latest articles, reports, and insights from leading industry sources to keep professionals, investors, and enthusiasts informed about developments in offshore energy.

Our Focus Areas

  • Deepwater Drilling: Ultra-deepwater operations, drilling rigs, drillships, and semi-submersibles
  • Gulf of America Operations: OCS leasing, BOEM/BSEE updates, and regional developments
  • Crude Oil & Natural Gas: Production data, pricing trends, and market analysis
  • Subsea & Pipelines: Infrastructure projects, flowlines, and subsea systems
  • LNG & Integrated Gas: Export terminals, FLNG, and gas processing
  • Wells & Exploration: Discoveries, well completions, and prospect updates

How It Works

Deepwater Daily is powered by a custom Python script that:

  • Fetches RSS feeds from major oil & gas news sources and industry publications
  • Filters articles for deepwater drilling and Gulf of America relevant content
  • Categorizes content by topic for easy navigation
  • Updates daily to ensure you have the latest industry news

Data Sources

We aggregate content from trusted industry sources including:

  • Offshore Magazine
  • Rigzone
  • World Oil
  • Oil Price
  • Offshore Technology
  • Oil & Gas Journal
  • S&P Global Commodity Insights
  • BOEM & BSEE (Bureau of Ocean Energy Management & Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement)
  • EIA (Energy Information Administration)
  • Major operator news (ExxonMobil, Chevron, Shell, BP)

Updates

The data on this site is updated daily. Each update brings fresh content from our curated sources, ensuring you never miss important industry developments.
